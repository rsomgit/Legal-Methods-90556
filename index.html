Legal Methods

Course Description



The course is organized into three modules. The first module introduces students to basic legal reasoning skills, including reading and analyzing cases, identifying the key elements of a legal decision, and preparing case briefs. The second module shows students how cases fit together and how precedents are applied to new fact scenarios. The third module provides tips on exam preparation and writing. The course also provides general background information on the foundations of the Canadian legal system.
